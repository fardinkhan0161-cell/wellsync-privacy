WellSync Privacy Policy

Your health data stays with you. Always.

Last Updated: June 26, 2026

🔒 Key Point: 100% Local Storage

WellSync does not collect, transmit, upload, or share any of your personal health information to any external servers, cloud services, or third parties. All data is stored locally on your device only.

1. Information We Collect

WellSync is a personal health tracking application. The only information we "collect" is the data you voluntarily enter into the app, which includes:

  • Symptom entries (type, severity, mood, triggers, notes)
  • Blood pressure readings (systolic, diastolic, pulse)
  • Blood sugar readings (value, meal timing, unit)
  • Sleep logs (hours, quality, notes)
  • Walking/activity data (step count, distance)
  • BMI calculations (weight, height, gender)

2. How We Store Your Data

Local Storage Only

All data entered into WellSync is stored exclusively on your device's local storage using AsyncStorage. We do not use:

  • Cloud servers or databases
  • Remote APIs for data storage
  • Analytics or tracking services
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Third-party data processors

Data Persistence

Your data remains on your device until you:

  • Manually delete individual entries within the app
  • Clear the app's data through your device settings
  • Uninstall the application

⚠️ Important Notice

If you uninstall WellSync, all locally stored data will be permanently lost unless you have exported it using the app's built-in CSV or PDF export feature. We cannot recover your data as we never have access to it.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not share, sell, or disclose your data to anyone.

The only way your data leaves your device is when you explicitly choose to:

  • Export a PDF report to share with your healthcare provider
  • Export a CSV file for personal record-keeping
  • Use the system share sheet to send files via email, messaging, etc.

These exports are initiated and controlled entirely by you. We do not see, process, or transmit this data.

4. Health Sync Integration

WellSync offers optional integration with Google Fit for importing health data (steps and sleep). If you choose to connect:

  • Authentication is handled directly between your device and Google's servers
  • We do not store your Google account credentials
  • Synced data is stored locally alongside your manual entries
  • You can disconnect at any time from the Sync tab

5. Security

While we store data locally and do not transmit it, your data security depends on your device's security:

  • Use device lock (PIN, pattern, fingerprint, Face ID)
  • Keep your operating system updated
  • Do not root or jailbreak your device
  • Be cautious when granting app permissions

6. Children's Privacy

WellSync is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has used the app, please contact us.

7. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your entries in the app
  • Export: Download PDF or CSV reports anytime
  • Delete: Remove individual entries or clear all data
  • Correction: Edit any entry through the History tab

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of WellSync after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
